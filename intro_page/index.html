🧭 How Uniflow Works

1

Create Your Career ID

Sign up and instantly generate your Uniflow Career ID. Add your branch, CGPA, year, skills, and interests. This profile becomes your passport to every opportunity.

2

Explore Career Zones

Access 3 live zones — Internships, Placements, and Hackathons. See ongoing, upcoming, and even past listings with start dates, deadlines, and eligibility status.

3

Know Where You Stand

For each opportunity, Uniflow shows whether you’re eligible — and why or why not. Instantly see missing skills, CGPA gaps, or branch restrictions. No more guesswork.

4

Apply or Upskill

Match? Apply in one click. Not eligible? Get guided skill recommendations to close the gap. See what’s required and start preparing — no external tools needed.

5

Track & Reflect

View the full timeline of past hackathons — including problem statements and company tracks — to practice or build portfolios. Analyze your application history with real insights.

6

Update Anytime

Your profile evolves with you. Add new skills, certificates, projects, and career goals. Uniflow auto-adapts your matches as you grow.
